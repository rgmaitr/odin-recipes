Teriyaki Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Description

Juicy shrimp marinated in cumin, Italian seasoning, lemon, garlic, pepper flakes, and fresh herbs, then threaded with vibrant flavored veggies. This quick dinner is one explosive yet quick and easy, busy weeknight meal. And it's easily customized!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp
  • 1/2 fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • skewers
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (Optional)

Hamburgers with Grilled Apple and Chili Crisp

Description

Apple burgers consist of patties made with a mixture of pork, beef, and shredded apples. Ingredients added to enhance the flavor are fresh sage, brown sugar, dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. The fresh sage adds a lightness and compliments the fruit throughout the burgers. And the brown sugar adds a little sweetness with dijon for a balanced flavor profile.
